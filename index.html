Process

Your AI Sales
Machine.

From storm alert to signed contract — RoofFlow handles every step between the lead and your crew showing up.

Capture every lead, instantly
Website forms, Google Ads, storm trackers, referrals — every source funnels into one live queue. Nothing slips through.
